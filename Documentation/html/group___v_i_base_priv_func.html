void VirtualInstrument.NormalizeAudioClips ( AudioClip  aAudioClips[][])
private

Normalizes the data from the audio clips and puts them in the mAudioData member variable.

Parameters
[in]aAudioClipsThe audio clips that were loaded for this instrument.

This function makes it so that the waveforms of the samples used for the instrument have a peak of -0.1. If Built-In Dynamics are supported, then the peak is multiplied by a ratio of the Built-In Dynamics thresholds. For example, waveforms that are used for velocities from 50-75 would have a peak that is 75% of the peak of the waveforms used for velocities from 76-100.
